TOUR TO NEPTUNE

Discovered only in 1846, Neptune will greet you with its blue and icy surface. And although the Earth is 30 times closer to the Sun than Neptune, our travel agency will be able to provide a trip to this giant planet.

The most distant, but not the most boring planet can surprise you no worse than others. Neptune is also named after the Roman god of the seas, and for good reason.

Tour information:

Visit the most extraordinary water park in the entire galaxy! Rolling on bright blue ice slides, you will observe mesmerizing landscapes. Unreal and colorful photos will be provided for you!

You can replace flights in a wind tunnel with flights on Neptune. The strongest winds are raging in the atmosphere. Their speed reaches 2000 km / h. Feel all the charm of flight and get unforgettable emotions!

You will get the chance to observe the system of 6 rings and 13 satellites of Neptune, which you will not see so clearly anywhere else!

Interesting facts about the planet

1. Earth's gravity is less than the gravity of Neptune by only 17%.

2. Neptune is 4 times heavier than our planet.

3. In the entire solar system, Neptune is the coldest planet. Its temperature in the upper atmosphere is close to −220 ° C.

4. A year on this planet lasts 90,000 days.

5. The speed of rotation of Neptune is almost 2 times faster than the speed of rotation of the Earth.

Way

Weather changes also occur on Neptune as they do on Earth, but the only difference is that each season lasts for about 40 long years.
