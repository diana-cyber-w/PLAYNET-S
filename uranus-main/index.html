TOUR TO URANUS

In 1986, Uranus was named the most boring planet in the solar system. But we will prove to you that this is absolutely not the case! The main thing is to be careful, because monstrous storms sometimes rage on the surface of Uranus, covering an area the size of America. But here is where to hide, because Uranus is about four times the size of Earth.

If the Earth was the size of an apple, then Uranus would be like a basketball. They say that the Blue Planet has such terrible weather because it has a cold heart: of all the giant planets in our system, only Uranus emits less heat than it receives from the Sun. But, even in spite of this, our tour will charge you with a good mood!

Tour information:

A very little studied and therefore mysterious Uranus is a planet that makes more than one generation of astronomers puzzled. What is this bluish ice giant? Our tour will reveal all the secrets!

You will be part of a unique excursion around the planet, do not miss your chance! Still, Uranus has been visited by a spacecraft only once. Your attention will be presented to the rings of the Blue Planet, which only a few know about! Uranus is the least studied planet in the entire solar system.

You can also visit any of the 27 satellites of Uranus! The largest, Titania, has a diameter of about half that of the Moon.
