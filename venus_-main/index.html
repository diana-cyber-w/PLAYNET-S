TOUR TO VENUS

Are you still afraid to travel to alien planets? Are you scared to fall into complete darkness or meet alien creatures?

Then we offer a tour to Venus. Most people associate this planet with love and passion.

Venus is a bright fireball and one of the hottest planets in the solar system, with a crazy atmosphere. Venus in terms of brightness in the sky is second only to the Sun and the Moon.

Tour information:

There is no change of seasons here, so if you like hot summer, then it is guaranteed to you! Traveling to Venus is better than any tanning bed, because the temperature can reach 480 degrees!

If you have a desire to visit Venus, then you will definitely not have to lose weight before the trip, because the magnetic field is so weak that we will weigh much less on the neighboring planet!

Fly out to check!

Venus Day is the dream of people who need hours in the day. The poets who glorified the goddess Venus considered the day spent with her as a year, and they were right, because the planet rotates around its axis in 243 Earth days, which means that one day on Venus lasts longer than an Earth year!

If you trust the coin and toss it up on Venus, then it will take a long time to make a decision, passing through the atmosphere like the thickness of our ordinary water.
