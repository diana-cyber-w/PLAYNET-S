TOUR TO SATURN

Apart from Earth, Saturn is the most recognizable planet in the solar system. The reason for this is obvious - the rings, but it is believed that Saturn will absorb them within 100 million years.

Have you seen them yet? Then we invite you to our incredible space tour.

This is one of the four gas giants, located 6th in order from the Sun. You will be really curious to know the planet Saturn!

Tour information:

Can't stand the cold winter? Or hate the sultry summer? One season on Saturn lasts over 7 years. And when the seasons change, the planet changes its color.

Saturn is the second largest planet in the solar system. And the mass exceeds the mass of our Earth by more than 95 times! And what's more interesting, the northern lights often appear on Saturn. How beautiful it is!

One of Saturn's most famous landmarks is the Hexagon, a hexagonal vortex above the planet's north pole. This unusual atmospheric phenomenon has never been observed anywhere else in the solar system. The dimensions of this hexagon are so vast that four Earths could fit inside it!

Today Saturn is the least explored planet. However, data on Saturn is constantly growing, and this happens thanks to the planetary mission "Cassini". The spacecraft is constantly monitoring not only the gas giant itself and its ring system, but also the satellites of the planet. But it is believed that more than 40% of the satellites of the solar system revolve around this planet.

Saturn is the only planet that rotates on its axis faster than around its orbit. Really dizzying!
