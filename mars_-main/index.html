TOUR TO MARS

It's no secret that Mars is the supposed alternative to Earth in the future.

Many are skeptical about this venture, and scientists argue on this topic. Why don't you make sure for yourself what is true and where is a lie?

If you think that Mars is boring - our tour will dispel all your doubts! And that the average temperature of the Red Planet is −55 ° C and the idea of ​​an atomic bombing has been proposed to warm it up? With our tour you will learn all the most unusual facts about this place! Let's reveal the main secret - is life on Mars real?

Tour information:

Mars is a red planet, turned out to be not at all what science fiction writers imagined it to be, it conceals thousands of unsolved mysteries ... Not yet solved! Hurry up, because on our tour you can:

Visit the largest volcano in the solar system; its diameter is 600 km, and its total area is 30 million km2 (which is comparable to the area of ​​Africa!). But don't worry, it won't take long, because with our modern rockets, we can fly around the entire volcano in just a couple of hours. Walk along the Mariner Valley canyon, which is much deeper and longer than the Great Canyon of the Earth. I wonder what we will find in the depths of an unknown planet?

How without conquering new heights! We will go on a fascinating journey to Mount Olympus, which is 27.4 kilometers high. This is three times the height of Everest!

And, of course, you can visit the satellites of the Red Planet - Deimos and Phobos, which are translated from Greek as "Fear" and "Horror", respectively. Due to the peculiarity of rotation, they are always turned to Mars with only one side. What do those hidden sides conceal in themselves?
