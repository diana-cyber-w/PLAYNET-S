MERCURY TOUR

Do you know that Mercury is not the hottest planet?

While on the day side of Mercury the temperature exceeds four hundred degrees, on the night side it can be below zero!

Therefore, you should not worry - our tour offers you to visit both the cold side and the hot side! Here you will not be bored, you can even hang in the clouds! Yes, you heard right - there are clouds at the poles of Mercury. There is also an opportunity to relax on a Small planet - geysers that periodically erupt will give an unforgettable experience!

Tour Information:

Despite the fact that the planet is almost unexplored, our tour will fill you with a lot of positive emotions! The "Plain of Heat" is the largest crater on Mercury. The entire surface of this planet is heavily dotted with impact craters, since after its formation Mercury was subjected to intense bombardment by asteroids and comets, so now it looks like the Moon.

Of course, this tour will give you the opportunity to relax! What could be more pleasant than relaxing on one of the hydrogen geysers! A tour of the canyons of Mercury, the depth of which can reach hundreds of kilometers! Don't be surprised if you see the stone core of the planet!

Facts about the planet:

1. Mercury is considered to be the most iron-rich planet in the universe.

2. Mercury is the only planet whose orbit is coplanar with the equator.

3. Mercury has no known moons.

Way

Mercury is one of the most difficult targets to study among the major planets. To get into orbit around it, the MESSENGER device took 6.5 years of flight, during which it performed six gravitational maneuvers and overcame 7.9 billion km. This is more than the distance between Earth and Pluto.
